Understanding Your PC

Don't buy a new computer just yet! Most PCs are modular, meaning you can swap individual parts to boost performance. Here is how it works.

🧠

RAM (Memory)

The "short-term" memory. Upgrading this allows you to run more Chrome tabs and apps at once without the PC stuttering.

Upgradeability: Easy / High
⚡

SSD (Storage)

Replacing an old Hard Drive with an SSD is the single best way to make an old computer feel brand new again.

Upgradeability: Moderate / High
🎮

Graphics Card (GPU)

Crucial for gaming and video editing. A simple swap here can double your frame rates in modern titles.

Upgradeability: Simple (Desktop Only)
🔳

Processor (CPU)

The brain of the PC. While upgradeable, it often requires a matching motherboard, making it a more complex surgery.

Upgradeability: Complex
